After a year of Virtual Monthly Meetups and lots of other discussions, it is time for us to finally STEP UP!

Presents

Develop non-commercial, open and long-term game projects together virtually led by the members of the community themselves where members contribute a little every week towards the game completion followed by its release at the time set initially by the lead.

No pressure to communicate or contribute! Join whatever game project interests you! Doesn't matter if you are a gamedev or not! Contribute in code, art, audio, whatever you would like to learn!

But why bother?

  1. To learn developing games in a collaborative and supportive environment.
  2. To learn any gamedev-related skill by 'doing' it without having to worry about other game elements.
  3. To learn leading a team, managing a game project, its scope and releasing it on time.
  4. To improve communication, soft skills, public speaking and the ethics of remote work.
